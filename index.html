An Bealach — Emergency Alert

If you are in danger or need help, press the button below.

How it works (60 seconds):

  1. Tap SEND EMERGENCY ALERT
  2. Choose your emergency contact
  3. Tap Share location
  4. Press Send

That’s it. Your location is sent immediately.

When to use

Walking · Running · Traveling · Hiking · Alone · Feeling unsafe

About An Bealach

An Bealach means “The Path” in Irish — a reminder that whatever road you’re on, you don’t walk it alone.
